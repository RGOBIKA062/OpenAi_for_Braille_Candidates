About Braille AI Assistant
Braille AI Assistant is designed to empower visually impaired users with seamless access to information, voice navigation, and interactive chat powered by advanced AI models. Our mission is to make technology accessible, intuitive, and inclusive for everyone.
  • Voice-driven navigation and commands
  • Accessible chat interface with Braille-friendly design
  • Instant answers and detailed explanations powered by Llama-3 AI
  • Text-to-speech for all responses
  • Extraordinary, modern UI for an engaging experience
